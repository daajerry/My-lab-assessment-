Career Aspirations

Why I'm doing this

My goal is to build a successful career in cybersecurity while making a positive impact on the lives of the community by protecting their privacy and digital assets. Every system I learn to defend represents real people — their savings, their conversations, their identities — and that responsibility is what keeps me motivated through every late night of coursework and lab work.
